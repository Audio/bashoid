- Bash: bash - prints random entry from http://bash.org
- Pepa: *bot's name*: *message_to_pepa* - Talk with pepa! (example: "Bashoid: Hi there!")
- RSS: rss list - list available channels || rss show *channel_name* - sends you log of last messages for that *channel_name*
- Stopwatch: stopwatch start, stopwatch stop, stopwatch get - stopwatch control
 -- stopwatch timer *time* *notify_period* - starts timer for *time* and will notify every *notify_period. Time can be in 4h30m4s format or just seconds
 -- stopwatch rmtimer - removes active timer
- Web page title: t *url* - shows content of *url*'s <title> tag
